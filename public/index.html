API Documentation

# Method Url Info
1 GET /q1 Get the first 10 phones sorted by ID in ascending order
2 GET /q2 Get the 5 most expensive phones
3 GET /q3 Get the 3 phones with the highest total score, skipping the first 3
4 GET /q4 Get the phone with the name "Mi 10"
5 GET /q5 Get all phones with "Adreno" in the GPU attribute
6 POST /q6 Add a new company {"id": "5","name": "LG","country": "korea"}
7 PUT /q7 Update the company "LG" and change the country to "SOUTH KOREA"
8 DELETE /q8 Delete the company with ID 5
9 GET /q9 Get the count of all phones
10 GET /q10 Get the count of phones with "Qualcomm" in the CPU attribute
11 GET /q11 Get phones priced between 1300 and 2000, limited to 4, sorted by price
12 GET /q12 Get phones with total scores of 86, 90, or 79
13 GET /q13 Get the first 10 phones sorted by company_id, showing only name and total_score
14 GET /q14 Get phones with battery_score of 76 or company_id of 2, sorted by price in descending order
15 GET /q15 Get phones with battery_score of 76 and company_id of 4
16 GET /q16 Get the count of phones grouped by company_id
17 GET /q17 Get the average price of phones grouped by company_id
18 GET /q18 Get the minimum price of phones grouped by company_id
19 GET /q19 Populate the company_id field in the phones collection
20 GET /q20 Get the 5 most expensive phones and populate the company_id field with the country from the companies collection
